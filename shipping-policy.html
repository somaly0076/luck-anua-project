Shipping policy


SHIPPING DETAILS


  • International orders are not accepted at the moment, as we only ship to the US. Also, non-contiguous US regions are
    undeliverable.
  • We use UPS for the courier service and the delivery time varies by location.
  • It usually takes about 6-7 business days on average, but duly note that there may be delays for unforeseen factors that occur during
    the shipment process.

SHIPPING COST


For US residents, a flat rate of $6.99 will be charged for shipping. However, please be noted that it may vary depending on the
region, such as Hawaii or Alaska.


Free shipping is offered for any orders above $60.


RECIPIENT'S ABSENCE/UNCLEAR ADDRESS


Please provide a physical address, as UPS does not ship to PO Box, FPO/DPO/APO addresses.


From time to time, a missed delivery or failed delivery attempt does happen due to your absence at the time of delivery or an
incomplete/unclear address.
In this case, the package will be returned to us, and you will receive a refund payment with the return fee deducted.
